## 1. Automation Kyun Zaruri Hai? (The Power of Automation)

Har business mein kuch fix kaam hote hain. Jaise:

* Jab deal finalize ho, toh automatically bill generate ho jaye.
* Jab naya customer aaye, toh use welcome email chala jaye.
* Data entry mein koi galti na ho.

**Mantra:** "Agar user ko sirf ek hi kaam karna hai, toh wo kaam computer se hi karwa do!" Isse aap apne organization ke "Superhero" ban sakte hain.

---

## 2. Automation ke Do Type (Categories)

Salesforce mein automation do tareekon se hota hai:

### A. Interactive Experiences (User ke saath milkar)

Yahan user ko screen par kuch dikhta hai ya wo button dabata hai.

* **Screen Flows:** Jaise koi form bharna ya sawal-jawab wali screen.
* **Autolaunched Flows:** Jab aap kisi button par click karte hain aur peeche koi kaam ho jata hai.
* **Approval Processes:** Jaise chutti (leave) mangna ya discount maangna, jahan senior ko 'Approve' ya 'Reject' button dabana hota hai.
* **Code (Lightning/Visualforce):** Jab standard tools se kaam nahi banta, toh developers HTML/JavaScript se custom design banate hain.

### B. Behind-the-Scenes Automation (Peeche se hone wala kaam)

Yahan user ko pata bhi nahi chalta aur kaam ho jata hai.

* **Record-Triggered Flows:** Jab koi record naya banta hai ya update hota hai (e.g., Address badalte hi manager ko alert jana).
* **Schedule-Triggered Flows:** Ek fixed time par chalne wala kaam (e.g., Har Monday subah report bhejna).
* **Platform Event/Data 360 Flows:** Jab data mein koi badlav ho (Data Cloud ka naya naam **Data 360** hai).
* **Apex Code:** Jab bahut complex calculation karni ho jo Flow se nahi ho sakti.

## 3. Quiz Ke Answers (Explaination ke saath)

Aapne jo quiz share kiya hai, uske sahi jawab ye hain:

**Q1: True or false: One benefit of automation is that it can improve data quality and consistency.**

* **Sahi Jawab:** **A. True**
* **Kyun?** Kyunki computer thakta nahi hai aur wo hamesha wahi rules follow karega jo aapne set kiye hain, isliye data hamesha sahi aur ek jaisa (consistent) rahega.

**Q2: Which type of automation runs at a specific date and time?**

* **Sahi Jawab:** **D. Schedule-triggered flow**
* **Kyun?** Iske naam mein hi "Schedule" hai. Jaise aap alarm lagate hain, waise hi ye flow kisi fix date ya time par apne aap chalta hai.
